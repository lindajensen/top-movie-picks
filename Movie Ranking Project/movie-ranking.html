My Top 3 Movies of All Time

THE BOONDOCK SAINTS

  • 1999
  • Action/Thriller
  • 1h 48min

"Boondock Saints" is a vigilante action thriller that follows to Irish Brothers who's trying to take down Boston's criminals. The film is a character driven ride filled with witty dialogue and satire. It has some truly thought-provoking moments, but is also home to one of the best F-bombs in movie history. For me, everything about this film is a cinematic party!

Cast

Willem Dafoe, Sean Patrick Flanery, and Norman Reedus

ARRIVAL

  • 2016
  • Drama/Sci-Fi
  • 1h 56min

"Arrival" is a one of the greatest sci-fi films of all time. The entire film is centered around the perception of time. It has an incredible story, characters, and an equally remarkable soundtrack. The film is beautiful and complex and even if you're not a sci-fi fan this is not a film you want to miss.

Cast

Amy Adams, Jeremy Renner, and Forest Whitaker

MILLION DOLLAR BABY

  • 2004
  • Drama
  • 2h 12min

”Million Dollar Baby” is about Maggie, a young working-class woman who is determined to become a boxer. This film can easily be mistaken for yet another cliché sports film, but it’s so much more. This isn’t a boxing film, it’s an heartbreakingly sad film about a boxer. It’s a raw and touching story that hits you right in the gut and stays with you long after the credits roll.

Cast

Hilary Swank, Clint Eastwood, and Morgan Freeman
